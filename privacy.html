Privacy Policy

Missed Image Viewer is designed to view and manage local images on your Mac. It does not collect, sell, or share personal data.

Data collection

The app does not run analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking systems. The website also does not use analytics, cookies, tracking pixels, or advertising scripts.

Local files and permissions

The app may ask for access to folders such as Desktop, Documents, Downloads, external drives, or file-provider folders so it can open selected images, browse nearby images, rotate images, and move images to Trash. These files stay on your Mac.

Purchases

Optional purchases are processed by Apple. Apple may handle purchase-related information according to Apple's own privacy policies and App Store terms.
